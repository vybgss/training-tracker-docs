Training Tracker does not require you to create an account or provide any personal identifiable information. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data related to your identity or personal contacts.

We may collect non-personally identifiable data related to the performance and usage of the app (such as crash logs or error reports) through Apple’s built-in analytics or other trusted frameworks. Training Tracker does not integrate with external services that collect personal data. Any analytics or diagnostic information is handled securely through Apple’s ecosystem (if you have opted in) and is anonymized.

Since we do not store personal data, you do not need to worry about private information being compromised. Local settings, match data, and scores you track remain on your device. We rely on Apple’s standard security and sandboxing measures to protect app integrity.

Training Tracker is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from children. Since we do not store personal data, there is no risk to child privacy within our app.
